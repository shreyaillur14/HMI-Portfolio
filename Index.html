Why HMI

HMIs bridge the gap between humans and machines, making complex systems easier to operate. They provide user-friendly interfaces with real-time data, allowing operators to monitor and control processes efficiently. This leads to increased productivity, reduced errors, and enhanced safety through early warning systems and data-driven insights. HMIs ultimately optimize system performance and operator experience.

Assignments

The MATLAB-based Vehicle Dashboard Simulation offers a realistic driving experience. It displays key vehicle parameters like speed, temperature, and battery voltage in real-time. Users can interact with virtual controls and receive alerts for critical conditions. This simulation serves as a valuable learning tool for understanding vehicle systems and dynamics.
